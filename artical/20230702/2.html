Meet the Asus Zenfone 10: A Compact Powerhouse with Flagship Features Tailored for Small Phone Enthusiasts!



Previous Next

In a world where smartphones are getting bigger and bulkier, Asus is breaking the mold with the introduction of the Asus Zenfone 10. Designed for those who prefer a compact device that doesn't compromise on performance, this smartphone is set to capture the hearts of small phone enthusiasts.


At the heart of the Asus Zenfone 10 is its remarkable 5.9-inch OLED display boasting a smooth 144 Hz refresh rate and a resolution of 2400×1080. This petite powerhouse measures just 146.5 mm×68.1mm×9.4 mm, making it one of the smallest phones available without sacrificing functionality.


Underneath its sleek exterior, the Asus Zenfone 10 packs a Qualcomm Snapdragon 8 gen 2 SoC, ensuring snappy performance and seamless multitasking. With a base configuration of 8GB of RAM and the option to upgrade to a staggering 16GB, users can expect effortless navigation and smooth operation. Storage options range from 128GB to 512GB, leveraging the lightning-fast UFS 4.0 technology.


Despite its compact size, the Zenfone 10 manages to incorporate several impressive features. It includes a 3.5-mm headphone jack, allowing users to enjoy their favorite music without the hassle of adapters or wireless headphones. Additionally, the device supports 15 W wireless charging (with wired charging reaching up to 30 W), offering convenient power replenishment. Other notable features include stereo speakers, NFC, IP68 dust and water resistance, Wi-Fi 7, and Bluetooth 5.3.


Photography enthusiasts will appreciate the Zenfone 10's camera capabilities. Equipped with a 50 MP Sony IMX766 main camera featuring a 1/1.56-inch sensor, users can capture stunning photos with exceptional clarity. Complementing the main camera is a 13 MP ultra-wide lens, perfect for capturing expansive landscapes. Selfie lovers will enjoy the 32 MP front-facing camera, delivering impressive self-portraits.


Asus has thoughtfully designed the Zenfone 10, showcasing a modern aesthetic with slim bezels and sleek metal side rails. The front display cover is protected by durable Gorilla Glass, while the back boasts a plastic construction, distinguishing itself from prevailing industry trends.


One unique design element of the Zenfone 10 is the power button, which serves a dual purpose. Acting as a side fingerprint reader, it provides secure and convenient device unlocking. When the phone is unlocked, the power button transforms into a touch-sensitive scroll area, allowing users to effortlessly navigate through vertically oriented content by swiping down.


The Asus Zenfone 10 is scheduled to release in Europe at the end of July, with a price tag of 799 euros (~$872). While the pricing for the US market is yet to be announced, the phone is expected to be compatible with T-Mobile and AT&T, offering users the opportunity to experience the power and convenience of this compact gem.


For those who yearn for a high-performing smartphone that fits snugly in their hand, the Asus Zenfone 10 emerges as an ideal choice. With its compact size, flagship features, and meticulous attention to detail, it caters to the needs of small phone enthusiasts while delivering an exceptional user experience.
